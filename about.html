A little about me.

I am a creative, data driven, and enthusiastic leader with a proven ability to solve complex problems with simple, elegant solutions.

Career Path

I started as a Program Manager out of college and worked hard to become a Sr. Technical Product Manager. I was then promoted to Manager, Program Management and eventually became an Engineering Manager leading all engineering disciplines working on Expedia’s Insurance technology team. Then I was given the opportunity to move into a traditional Product Management role where I identified and evaluated opportunities, formulated a product vision, and evangelized that vision to stakeholders.

These different roles have given me a unique, multi-disciplined understanding of the software development lifecycle that you will not find in most people.
